Krusty Krab

The Krusty Krab is the one and only restaurant in the Bikini Bottom that offers the delicious Krabby Pattie. Founded and owned by Eugene Krabs, it is the most successful restaurant in all of Bikini Bottom.

  • Krabby Pattie

    Krusty Krab's original best selling burger. It uses only the finest Bikini Bottom lettuce, tomatoes, cheese pickles, mustard and ketchup, and the Krabby Pattie secret formula, this delicate burger will definitely make you come back for more.

  • Krabby Combo

    Krusty Krab's original Krabby Pattie combined with a drink of your choice and a dessert. This combo will definitely relieve your hunger and is definitely worth your money.

  • Krabby Deluxe

    A twist on Krusty Krab's original Krabby Pattie, the Krusty Deluxe has two patties and the option to add toppings of your choice. This delicious burger will definitely make you eat like a seahorse.
